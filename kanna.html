Kanna is an Anime RPG and Economy Discord bot developed by Mirabellier for fun. You can invite it to your Discord server by joining the support server! You can also join our Discord server Kanna's Kawaii Klubhouse for all questions, suggestions, and assistance! It comes packaged with a variety of fun commands and utility commands that can be useful for your server.





Kanna's prefix is k. and you can use the prfix to type the following commands: k.help, k.country, k.emote, k.register, k.botinfo, k.roleinfo and much more! You can join the support server for more info.
